About The Summit

SWARM ORANGE SUMMIT 2018 brings together the Ethereum Foundation Swarm team members and core contributors in an environment of total immersion, with honorary guests representing projects and companies interested in swarm.

You are kindly invited to participate, submit talk proposals (target format: 30 minutes total - 20 minute presentation followed by Q&A), apply as a sponsor or volunteer to help or contribute in some other form. Places are limited so respond early.
Call for talks due date: April 20th, 2018

Scope: decentralised base layer infrastructure web3; decentralised storage and db, messaging, service networks and the future of the internet
Focus: SWARM
Target: Makers, Developers, Seekers & fans

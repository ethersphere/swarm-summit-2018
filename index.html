SOS1 - Swarm Orange Summit 2018

Swarm Orange Summit 2018 is happening in the picturesque city of Ljubljana*, Europe’s green capital.

7 -11 May, 2018 Ljubljana, Slovenia

About The Summit

SWARM ORANGE SUMMIT 2018 bringing together the Ethereum Foundation Swarm team members and core contributors in an environment of total immersion, with honorary guests representing projects and companies interested in swarm

You are kindly invited to participate, submit talk proposals (target format 3^3 mins = 2/3 + 1/3 Q&A), apply as a sponsor or volunteer to help or contribute in some other form. Places are limited so respond early.
Call for talks due date: April 20th, 2018

Scope: decentralised base layer infrastructure web3; decentralised storage and db, messaging, service networks and the future of the internet
Focus: SWARM
Target: Makers, Developers , Seekers & fans

Synopsis of the agenda:

  • 1 Day / 07 May
  • 2-4 Day / 08-10 May
  • 5 Day / 11 May
  • 6 Day / 12 May

Evening: opening with public ethereum meetup swarm update.

Talks from 10:00-15:00hrs ; From 15:00 hrs splinter groups, project work, discussions, hacking.

Team sessions and hackathon with demo at 16:00hrs.

Slovenia day-trip.
